Skein¶

skein /skān/ noun

  1. A quantity of yarn, thread, or the like, put up together, after it is taken from the reel.

Skein is a simple library and cli for deploying jobs on Apache YARN. While YARN has many features that make it powerful for developing resilient applications, these features also can make it difficult for developers to use.

Skein aims to be easy to use, providing just as much YARN as your project needs.
